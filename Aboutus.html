Mission

The mission of the Sherubtse United Nations (UN) Club is to cultivate global citizenship among students by promoting the core values of the United Nations, such as peace, human rights, sustainable development, and international cooperation. The club seeks to provide a platform for students to engage with global issues, raise awareness about the UNs initiatives, and encourage active participation in addressing both local and international challenges.

Vision

The vision of the Sherubtse United Nations (UN) Club is to nurture informed, responsible, and globally-minded students who actively contribute to a peaceful and sustainable world. The club aspires to create a platform where young leaders are empowered to engage in global issues, make meaningful contributions to international peace and security, and advocate for the principles of the United Nations.
